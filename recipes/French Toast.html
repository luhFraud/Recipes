French Toast

Description

French toast is a dish made of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and typlically milk, then pan fried.

This Frech toast recipe makes a delicious breakfast or brunch. Make this simple, yet perfect French toast recipe that everyone will love.

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar, honey or maple syrup
  • 1 teaspooon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 8 slices sandwich bread
  • butter

Instructions

  1. Whick togther eggs, milk, salt, vanilla, and cinnamon in a flat-bottom pie plate or baking dish. Place bread slices, one or two at a time, into the egg mixture and flip to make sure both sides of bread are will-coated.

  2. Melt butter in a large skillet or on a griddle. Place bread slices in skillet or on griddle and cook on medium heat until golden brown on each side, about 2-3 minutes.

  3. Serve immediately or keep warm in oven until ready to serve, but no longer than about 30 minutes.
